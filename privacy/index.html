Keypassword

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how informations and data is handled in Keypassword app.

1. Introduction

Keypassword is a privacy-focused password generator that helps you create secure, strong and unique passwords instantly - right on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no cloud. Just simple, secure password creation made easy.

2. Information Collection

- No Personal Information Collected: Keypassword does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.
- No Account Required: You can use the app without registering or providing any information.
- No Tracking: The app does not use analytics, cookies, or advertising frameworks.

3. Passwords

- All passwords are generated locally on-device.
- Passwords are never sent to servers and remain entirely under your control.
- If you copy a password, it is stored temporarily in your operating system clipboard, managed by the operating system.
- If you share a password, the operating system share sheet is used.
- Keypassword does not access, monitor, or control what happens after you share.

4. Permissions

- Clipboard Access: Used only when you tap “Copy”.
- Share Sheet Access: Triggered only when you choose to share a password. This is handled entirely by the operating system, and the app never see or store the shared content.

5. Data Sharing

No data or info is shared with third parties.

6. Security

As no data is collected from anyone, this app is safe for users of all ages.
- Passwords are generated using system randomness directly on-device.
- No data or info is transmitted outside the device.
- For best security, it is recommend pasting copied passwords directly into a secure password manager and clearing your clipboard when finished.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any modification may be made without the user's prior or subsequent consent or notice. Changes will be reflected by updating this webpage.
